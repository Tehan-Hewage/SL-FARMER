Our Story

Meet Our Founder

The passion behind SL Farmer

Rashmi Perera

Founder & Head Farmer

With over 15 years of experience in sustainable agriculture, Rashmi founded SL Farmer with a vision to provide premium, naturally grown produce while preserving traditional farming methods.

Her dedication to organic farming and commitment to quality has made SL Farmer a trusted name in premium pineapples and ginger. Coming from a family of farmers, she combines traditional wisdom with modern sustainable practices.

"Our mission is simple: grow the finest pineapples and ginger using methods that respect both nature and our customers' health. Every fruit we harvest carries the love and care of our farming heritage."
