Our Story

Meet Our Founder

The passion behind SL Farmer

Jayasanka Peiris

Founder - SL Farmer

Rooted in more than a decade of hands-on experience in sustainable agriculture, Jayasanka Peiris created SL Farmer with a simple purpose - to grow premium, naturally cultivated produce while honoring the farming traditions passed down through generations.

Raised in a family of farmers, Jayasanka blends ancient agricultural wisdom with modern sustainable innovations, crafting a farming model that is both environmentally responsible and exceptionally productive. His passion for organic methods and unwavering commitment to quality have transformed SL Farmer into a renowned name for premium pineapples, ginger, and other naturally grown crops.

Guided by purpose, driven by heritage, and committed to purity - Jayasanka continues to lead SL Farmer toward a future where sustainability and excellence grow side by side.
